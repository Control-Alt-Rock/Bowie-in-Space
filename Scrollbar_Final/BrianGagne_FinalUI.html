Ustus Irons happens upon a taboo forum in the dark web and witnesses an image so volatile, it permanently scars his face. Now, a broken man, Ustus becomes reclusive and wears a scrollbar mask in order to warn others of scrolling down too far on a web page, and in turn, learning more about people. While working at a printing press, Ustus catches the eye of Silvio Bara, who begins developing romantic feelings towards the scrollbar obsessed miscreant. With Silvio's help, Ustus opens up his heart and begins to socialize more with his fellow pressman. Could the tool that once scarred him, also serve to replenish his social standing? Watch the dramatic transition from a bland two dimensionl character, into a fully realized three dimensional archetype.
